Our Story

DURAI Document Converter was born from a simple observation: people need a fast, secure, and reliable way to convert their text into various document formats without compromising their privacy. In today's digital age, document conversion shouldn't require uploading sensitive information to unknown servers or installing complex software.

Founded in 2025, DURAI represents a commitment to simplicity, security, and excellence. Our platform processes everything directly in your browser, ensuring that your data never leaves your device. This approach combines the power of modern web technologies with an unwavering commitment to user privacy.

What started as a personal project has evolved into a comprehensive document conversion solution used by professionals, students, and organizations worldwide. We believe that powerful tools should be accessible to everyone, which is why DURAI is completely free to use.

To become the world's most trusted document conversion platform, recognized for innovation, reliability, and unwavering commitment to user privacy and data security.
